## [[1.2.0]] - 2024-05-20

### Changed

- **Breaking**: Changed `meta::Runtime` date from `Date` to `String`



## [[1.1.3]] - 2024-04-30

### Added

## [[1.1.2]] - 2024-04-30

### Added

## [1.1.0] - 2024-04-20

- Add `ArticleTable`, `ImageTable`, `Tag`, `TagTable`, `TagsTable`, `User`, and `Email`.
